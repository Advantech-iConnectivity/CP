Industrial Managed Switch Certification Program

This certification program validates the skills needed for network architect positions. It’s a starting point for Advantech Channel Partner & RBU to have the knowledge and skills to plan, install, configure, and even troubleshoot the network architectures based on customer’s application.
Joining this certification program means you will have what it takes to manage and troubleshoot a designed network for the customers. The overall program is broken down into three levels:

- iConnectivity Product Basic (IPB)
- iConnectivity Certified Industrial Associate (ICIA)
- iConnectivity Certified Industrial Profession (ICIP)

IPB certification level is your first step to build up the basic required network knowledge including OSI defined networking and our product profolio to help you stand out from other distributor in the market.
As advanced certificate levels of ICIA and ICIP, you will be benefited with learning EKI-7700/9600/9700 Managed Switch Series related domain-knowledge in a systematic manner. Including digging out the operation for supported protocols and product configuration guides, it prepares you with the capability of how our products can be applied and also the troubleshooting skills.

There will be an official test for qualifying the selected program. Most importantly, the qualifying progress isn’t just self-training and paper-test, we include lot of hands-on labs in the materials and also in the tests for you to really get familiar with constructing the network environment.
The Self-Evaluation session after each study material is designed for participants to self-validate and conduct the related domain-knowledge among every topic before taking the official tests. Exam preview could be referred to the Take Exam page for participants to know how the question will look like when taking the exam.
