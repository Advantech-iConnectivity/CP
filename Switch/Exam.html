How to Take the Exams

The certification exam consists of two parts:
- Online Paper Written based Exam
- Practical Hands-on based Exam

Online Paper Written based Exam
- Examination link will be provided by Advantech in online style.
- A web browser is required to take the exam.
- Click HERE to get more details about how participant carry out the written exam.

Pratical Hands-on based Exam
- Participant should confirm all requested H/W device (i.e. refer to “Device Preparation Items” section) before proceeding to hands-on exam.
- The result of hands-on exam must also be completed and send back to responsible coach with a ".zip" file before the deadline.
- Click HERE to get more details about how participant carry out the hands-on exam.

※Exam preview could be referred to the Resources page for participants to know how the question will look like when taking the exam.

Certification Exam Result

Advantech will receive participants’ result after the exam is submitted. The testing score and analyzed form shown as the following figure will be announced after one week. Participants will know the score and weaker part of this certification level. Participants can base on this analyzed result to examine the learning state for focusing on weaker part and review learning module accordingly.

Please note that the answers of the certification exam will not be announced to the participants.

Recertification Exam Details

For enhancing product function and provide better customer satisfactions, the new features or new models may be released from time to time. The certification will be expired after 2 years.

We strongly suggest each participant to retake the exam every 2 years to keep following up with the product function and features. The recertification exam contents will focus on new features, and supplement by old features.
