Recertification Exam Details

For enhancing product function and provide better customer satisfactions, the new features or new models may be released from time to time. The certification will be expired after 2 years.

We strongly suggest each participant to retake the exam every 2 years to keep following up with the product function and features. The recertification exam contents will focus on new features, and supplement by old features.
